The XGSLab Advantage

Physics-based earthing & EMF platform for modern networks

XGSLab is a state-of-the-art software suite for the analysis of grounding systems, lightning effects, electromagnetic interference and EMF. It is built on advanced numerical methods (Boundary Element Method and Finite Element / Finite Difference approaches) and supports complex, multilayer soils and frequency-dependent behaviour.

  1. 1

    End-to-end grounding & EMF platform

    Four main modules ‚Äì GSA, GSA-FD, XGSA-FD and XGSA-TD ‚Äì cover low-frequency grounding, frequency-domain behaviour, 3D field computation and time-domain transients. Auxiliary modules extend this to soil resistivity analysis, seasonal effects, Fourier analysis and more.

  2. 2

    Accurate soil & conductor modelling

    Model uniform, multilayer and multizone soils, cable screens, rails, pipelines and overhead lines with frequency-dependent parameters. This allows realistic calculation of touch/step voltages, potential rise, interference and EMF.

  3. 3

    Standards-aligned design workflow

    XGSLab helps engineers work in line with international standards such as IEEE 80, IEC 61936-1, EN 50522 and related guides, producing outputs that can be directly used in safety and compliance documentation.

  4. 4

    Powerful visualisation & reporting

    Generate 2D and 3D plots of potentials, fields and currents; export charts and contour maps; and create clear reports for designers, safety engineers and regulators ‚Äì all directly from the XGSLab environment.

  5. 5

    CAD / GIS friendly

    Import geometry from DXF, KML and other formats, align models with site layouts and reuse templates across projects ‚Äì reducing manual drafting effort and improving consistency.

  6. 6

    Flexible licensing for teams

    Choose between single-user and network licences to match your team structure. XGSLab is designed for both small specialist groups and large engineering organisations working across many projects.

  7. 7

    Built for real-world projects

    XGSLab is used worldwide by utilities, railways, industrial plants and consulting engineers on live projects ‚Äì from transmission substations and HVDC links to pipelines, traction systems and renewable plants.
