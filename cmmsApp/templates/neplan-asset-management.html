Overview

NEPLAN Asset Management

The NEPLAN Asset Management Tool provides asset managers with powerful capabilities to model any system with utmost flexibility, answering critical "what happens if…" scenarios. It enables the detailed evaluation of each asset individually, allowing for the development of medium- and long-term strategies that optimize investment decisions, maintenance planning, and power supply reliability-ensuring cost efficiency without compromising performance.

Built on a modular and customizable framework, the software supports both Operational Asset Management and Strategic Asset Management needs. It is available across multiple platforms, including a desktop application, a cloud-based web browser solution, an intranet setup, and as a web service, offering organizations the flexibility to integrate it seamlessly into their existing IT environments.

Functionalities

  • Modern User Interface

    Intuitive, user-friendly design with remote control via web services and integrated maps for enhanced visualization.

  • Data Warehouse

    Import assets across industries (networks, power plants, oil, real estate), create custom or standard data interfaces, enable automatic updates, and manage clients and access rights efficiently.

  • Custom Data Views

    Visualize complex equipment hierarchies (e.g., substations, oil-refining plants) with user-defined grouping, filtering, data quality checks, and enrichment capabilities.

  • RCM (Reliability-Centered Maintenance)

    Flexible assessment systems for maintenance planning, budget optimization, hierarchical asset evaluation, comprehensive reporting, and easy Office export options.

  • Asset Simulation

    Run simulations for individual or grouped assets, analyze scenarios, compare strategies, perform budgeting, sensitivity analysis, and optimization for data-driven decision-making.

Asset Management

RCM & Asset Simulation / LCC

RCM

Reliability-Centered Maintenance

  • Evaluate the condition and importance of any asset category-including complex hierarchical assets such as substations.
  • Supports: TBM (Time Based Maintenanace), CBM (Condition Based Maintenance), RCM (Reliability Based Maintenance)
  • Integrated budget-planning tools ensure efficient scheduling of maintenance tasks.
  • Flexible export options to PDF, Microsoft Word, Excel,… for reporting.
LCC

Asset Simulation / Life-Cycle Cost

  • Compare medium- to long-term asset strategies and measure performance using technical and financial metrics.
  • Perform LCC analysis for individual or grouped assets with dynamic, time-dependent attributes.
  • Scenario planning, parameter sensitivity, and dynamic measure adjustments provide comprehensive support for decision-making.
