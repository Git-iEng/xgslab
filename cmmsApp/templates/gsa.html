GSA · Grounding System Analysis

Design & Validate Safe Earthing Systems

GSA is the XGSLab module dedicated to earthing/grounding systems in low-frequency conditions. Model grids and electrodes, compute touch/step voltages, and verify compliance to IEEE/IEC/EN using multilayer soil models and realistic return paths.

Capabilities

What you can do with GSA

Fast, defendable grounding studies for substations, plants, renewables and rail.

Grid & Electrode Modelling

  • Buried meshes, rods, rings, foundations & earth plates
  • Touch/step voltages, GPR, current distribution
  • Bonding, transfer potentials & interface checks

Multilayer Soil & Measurements

  • SRA/SSA tools to fit 1–n layer soil models
  • Seasonal/resistivity variations and what-if cases
  • Improved accuracy for safety criteria

Standards & Reporting

  • IEEE Std 80, IEC/EN 50522, IEC 60479 checks
  • Mitigation (surface layers, gradient control)
  • Engineering-grade plots & compliance summaries

Scope & Technical Coverage

AreaDetails
Geometry Arbitrary meshes, interconnected grids, rods, plates, foundations, rings, shields, and connections to structures or third-party systems.
Soil Model Uniform or multilayer soils derived from Wenner/Schlumberger tests; seasonal/resistivity scenarios; automated curve fitting.
Results GPR, touch/step voltages, current density, conductor currents, surface potential maps, dangerous points and transfer potentials.
Compliance Safety evaluation against IEEE Std 80 / IEC-EN 50522 / IEC 60479 with mitigation aids (surface layer, additional bonds, gradient control).
Integration CAD-friendly import/export; consistent workflow with GSA_FD, XGSA_FD/TD, NETS and SHIELD for end-to-end studies.
