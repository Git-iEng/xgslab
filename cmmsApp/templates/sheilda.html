What You Can Do with SHIELD A

From manual trial-and-error to automated, standards-driven lightning protection layouts.

Automatic Layout Search

  • Optimizes number, height, and position of air-terminations
  • Respects keep-out, roof zones, and height/spacing limits
  • Targets full coverage with minimal hardware

Standards & Methods

  • Rolling Sphere & Eriksson methods for zone validation
  • Supports LPS Classes I–IV (IEC/EN 62305)
  • Instant coverage metrics and unprotected area checks

Scenarios & Deliverables

  • Batch scenarios by LPS class, mast height, and constraints
  • 3D visuals of protected volumes and strike interception
  • Bill-of-materials and layout drawings for implementation
