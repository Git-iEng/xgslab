EMI / Interference

Predict and mitigate interference into rails, pipelines, telecoms and controls.

Study areas

  • Inductive & conductive coupling: HV lines → third-party utilities.
  • Cathodic protection interactions: Co-located pipelines and earthing systems.
  • Rail traction effects: Return current paths, touch/step on platforms & fences.
  • Telecoms & signalling immunity: Field levels vs equipment limits.
  • Mitigations: Separation, bonding, cable shields, decouplers, gradient control mats.

Data & workflow

  • Intake: single-line, site layout, soil/boring logs, fault levels, CP/rail data if applicable.
  • Model build in XGSLab with quality checks and sensitivity runs.
  • Review workshop to agree mitigations before finalization.

Deliverables

  • Engineering report with assumptions, results, compliance checks and recommendations.
  • XGSLab project files, soil model fits, and result plots (touch/step, potentials, fields).
  • Construction drawings/details for grid layout, bonds, SPDs and mitigation hardware.
